Add `collapseOptions`, `img`, and `disableWhenUnchecked` props to `Checkbox`. `collapseOptions` renders a collapsible `Collapse.Trigger`/`Collapse.Panel` beneath the checkbox row, `img` displays an image beside the label, and `disableWhenUnchecked` disables the collapse while the checkbox is unchecked.
